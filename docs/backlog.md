# Backlog

Future-state work, phrased as destinations. Items graduate into plans; nothing here is a
commitment to order.

## Roguelite

- Meta progression across runs: a cross-slot ledger (unlocked module templates, recipes,
  starting-hull variants) layered additively on the save-is-one-run model (ADR-0004).
- Module drafting: choose one of N offered room modules after clearing a site.
- Site biome vocabulary: chunk families with distinct feedstock taps, terrain profiles,
  and enemy ecologies per biome.
- Run map: player chooses the next docking site from a branching route.

## Systems

- Free-form graft placement (edge-snap beyond hardpoints) if the hardpoint envelope
  proves too confining.
- Hull damage and repair as a site-defense consequence.
- Additional reagent chains and enemy counters built on the site-feedstock model.
